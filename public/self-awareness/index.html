Introduction. Self-awareness

Self-awareness is a skill that is designed to provide you with control.

To slow down your mind.

To dissolve what’s currently active in your mind.

To change your mind’s focus and act with intention.

The problem with mental illness is that we lose the ability to the control our mind.

We live with imbalance for so long that we assume that this is how we’ve always been.

Self-awareness aims to change that.

Self-awareness asks that you do not assume, nor accept.

Instead, it trains you to see.

Because isn’t that there’s anything wrong with you.

The problem is that due to the imbalance in your brain, you’re no longer able to see things clearly.

With awareness we can learn to see clearly again.
