This is your brain.

Each line represents a thought, an opinion or a judgement.

Providing us with direction.

Which in turn, helps us make decisions.

Allowing us to do all sorts of cool things.

You’re lame.

On the contrary, this is clarity.

Clarity itself doesn’t do much alone.

However when combined with direction, allows us to single out our thoughts, opinions and judgements.

In order to make informed decisions.

…or whatever it is you want from life.

Clarity is important because it prevents direction from taking over.

As well as from disappearing completely.

Basically.

A balance of both clarity and direction is required in order to function sustainably.


Too little direction.

(and we may feel helpless, lost or confused).

Too much direction.

(and we are left irrational and obsessive).


And if we maintain these imbalances for too long?

Well, we can end up developing mental illness.


Which can make life incredibly difficult, if not entirely unbearable.

Thankfully, mental illness is merely one configuration.

Instead, what this course will focus on is developing another potential option.

Balance.

In order to regain a balance of both direction and clarity.
