Welcome.

Every single thought and emotion we experience.

Is defined by what we know.

If we know that we are ugly.

Then it will define how we view ourselves.

A property of knowledge is that it is dynamic and ever-changing.

Problems can arise however when we mismanage this dynamic.

An excess or deficiency of the both benefitial and detrimental knowledge.

Can lead to imbalance.

Mental illness is the result of the mismanagement of this knowledge.

While knowledge itself can be empowering, it can also be detrimental.

Mental illness is the result of knowing those detrimental behaviours.

And when it is all we know, it can end up defining our entire human experience.

This website aims to assist people with mental health issues.

By utilising the power of explanation.

To challenge and redfine what we know.

Not only so we can know more about being in control over our happiness.

But also so we can learn to know less of what contributes towards our mental illness.
